Web development course

I took a part-time web development course at neue fische from October 2023 to May 2024. Below are shown pages of my course completion certificate.

During the course, I built a web application as a final course project together with a team of 2 other students. The application is called PlotData and is deployed with Vercel.
I came up with the concept for the project and let the team through its implementation.

The idea of PlotData is: a non-technical user can make a publication-quality graph of their data without needing to code. The processus is the following: first to upload a csv data file, then to customize the graph through different steps, finally to generate a graph which can then be downloaded as a png image. This image can then be used in reports / presentations.

The PlotData-the-2nd project is a fork of PlotData, which I haved further improved on my own, after the end of the course.
